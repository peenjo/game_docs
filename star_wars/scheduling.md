### Scheduling

To simplify (I hope) the scheduling issue, I've created an online calendar to track who and when for gaming sessions. Tools like Doodle are quick and easy, but it's hard to update your information when you change your mind. This allows anyone to edit the information without having to track logins, passwords, etc. Click on this:

[Calendar.Online](https://calendar.online/530ce9c7174d677bfed3)

There are four calendars :

- **Available** for upcoming sessions you are available
- **Not Available** for upcoming sessions you are not available
- **Playing** when we are playing and who will attend
- **Not Playing** when there are no game sessions

Mostly you'll be using the **Available** and **Not Available** calenders for the upcoming month or so. Click on the event for a given day, add your name to the "Who" field and that's it. Anyone can edit anything, so be mindful. 

If you are the first to edit a recurring event you will see some question about updating just this event, all events, etc. **Please just update the single event.**

Every so often (like when we talk about it during a session) I'll consolidate the Available/Not Available information and create the Playing/Not Playing events for the upcoming month or so. 

Events with specific times (mostly the Playing calendar) _should_ display in your local timezone. It will specify the 'local' timezone at the bottom right of the screen, which you can change if you need to.

That's it!
