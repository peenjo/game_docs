The group played a role-playing game called Mausritter, where they took on the roles of mouse characters from different tribes. #### Mouse Champions Investigate Blight Threat
The group of mouse champions from different tribes has been summoned to investigate the return of the Blight, which threatens their lands. Their champion, Olive Wigglebottom, went missing while investigating the resurgence of the disease. The group plans to travel to the Great Oak in the woods to find the missing Blue Jay and gather information from the owl that lives there. They discuss strategies for approaching the owl, considering its behavior and the dangers of traveling at night. The group decides to travel during the day to avoid predators and reach the woods by nightfall. They also consider using spells and abilities to protect themselves and gather information.
#### Owl Negotiation Strategy Discussion
The group discussed their approach to encountering an owl at the Great Oak. They decided to hide in the leaves at the end of a branch rather than entering the owl's nest. Catey, who had been made invisible and given the ability to understand the owl, attempted to negotiate with the owl, Sable Wing, from a distance. Despite her efforts to flatter the owl, it remained skeptical of her courage. The group considered using psychology to determine if the owl was lying about having seen a blue jay. They also discussed the possibility of using invisibility to sneak into the owl's nest, though this would require another spell cast.
