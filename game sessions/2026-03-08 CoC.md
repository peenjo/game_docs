#### Corin's Situation and Reverend's Plan  
The group discussed encountering a young girl named Corin who was running through fields, being chased by other children. They brought her to Reverend Earl Wilson, who explained that the town's children have been acting wild since their teacher left two weeks ago. The Reverend revealed that Corin's family, the Strands, had moved into the Bellwether house after the death of Agnes Bellwether, and that both the Strand family and Sheriff James McLaren had all died recently, leaving Corin as the only survivor. The Reverend offered to leave Corin in Catey's care while he travels to Wilmington for three days, as he was planning to take her with him initially but worried about showing her to officials before addressing the situation.  
#### Mysterious Child Figure Investigation  
The group discussed investigating a mysterious child-sized figure seen near the farm. Catey and Silas decided to stay with Corin while Sebastian and Robert went to investigate the woods where the figure was spotted. During the investigation, Robert and Sebastian encountered difficulties with the game mechanics for the "Spot Hidden" skill. Meanwhile, Catey and Silas spoke with Corin, who appeared scared and suggested the group should leave town. Corin drew pictures to describe a conflict between entities with fingers/tentacles coming from the sky and underground, possibly representing bugs and tentacles fighting each other.

#### Gazette Investigation and Mystery Map  
The group discussed their plan to investigate the Gazette and explore an apartment above a closed store. They successfully entered the apartment and found a cryptic map with blood spatter and a female fingerprint, which they photographed for analysis. The map appeared to reference "Broken Hill" and may be related to a potential dungeon or hive location. The group also learned from Richard Wendell that several people in town, including the sheriff and school teacher, had disappeared under suspicious circumstances, with only the doctor and deputy remaining.  
#### McLaren Farm Security Strategy Meeting  
The group discussed their strategy for dealing with Sheriff Cutter, ultimately deciding to avoid confrontation and leave town. They observed that the Iron Bridge had been destroyed and noticed suspicious children watching them from a distance. The group then arrived at the McLaren farmhouse, where Sam Morrison demonstrated a series of security measures including tripwires and booby traps around the property, particularly near the fuel bunker and in the surrounding woods. Sam warned that these defenses would be especially effective at night, coinciding with the upcoming full moon.

#### Cave Mission Strategy Planning
